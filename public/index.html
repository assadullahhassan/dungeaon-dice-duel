Goal

Reduce opponent HP to 0 before yours reaches 0.

Dice and powers

Each hero has Attack Power, Defense Power, and Max HP. Every round the server rolls four independent dice:

  • Attack roll for each side: a random integer from 1 up to and including that hero's Attack Power.
  • Defense roll for each side: a random integer from 1 up to and including that hero's Defense Power.

Damage

Damage is never negative; if the calculation goes below zero, it counts as 0.

  • Damage to the opponent = your attack roll - opponent's defense roll.
  • Damage to you = opponent's attack roll - your defense roll.

Both amounts are applied in the same round. HP cannot go below 0. When one side reaches 0 HP, the battle ends (if both reach 0 in the same round, it is a draw).
